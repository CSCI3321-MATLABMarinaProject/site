Tutorials / Other Resources on MATLAB

The following is a list of relevant tutorials and other resources that we have found to be relevant and useful. If you have any additional resources, please send an email to the Project Director with the URL and other information so we can include it in this list.

  1. Clarkson University-New York, Department of Mathematics: http://www.cyclismo.org/tutorial/matlab/
  2. MATLAB Tutorials: http://www.matlabtutorials.com/
  3. Southern Illinois University, Department of Mathematics: http://www.math.siu.edu/matlab/tutorials.html
  4. Matlab Summary and Tutorial, University of Florida: http://www.math.ufl.edu/help/matlab-tutorial/
  5. University of Utah: http://www.math.utah.edu/lab/ms/matlab/matlab.html
  6. University of Maryland, Computer methods in chemical engineering; MATLAB Tutorial: http://terpconnect.umd.edu/~nsw/ench250/matlab.htm
  7. This tutorial is available as a supplement to the textbook Fundamentals of Signals and Systems Using Matlab by Edward Kamen and Bonnie Heck, published by Prentice Hall (Georgia Institute of Technology): http://users.ece.gatech.edu/~bonnie/book/TUTORIAL/tutorial.html
  8. Control Tutorials for MATLAB (Carnegie Mellon): http://www.engin.umich.edu/group/ctm/basic/basic.html
  9. A practical introduction to MATLAB by Mark S. Gockenbach: http://www.math.mtu.edu/~msgocken/intro/intro.html
  10. West Virginia University . MATLAB Tutorial by Bohe Wang: http://www.csee.wvu.edu/~trapp/wvumatlab.htm
  11. MATLAB & Simulink . A Tutorial by Tom Nguyen: http://edu.levitas.net/Tutorials/Matlab/index.html
  12. MATLAB Tutorial by Kristian Sandberg, University of Colorado, Boulder: http://amath.colorado.edu/computing/Matlab/Tutorial/

An exhaustive list of tutorials is also found on the MathWorks web-site at http://www.mathworks.com/academia/student_center/tutorials/launchpad.html
